## Session 12: In the New City -- The New Creation

As we have studied the Bible together we have met a God who has come down into the garden, the tent, the temple, in the flesh and in the Spirit. Now as we look at the last book of the Bible, there is a sense of anticipation - for God is coming down to dwell with his people in the New Jerusalem.
