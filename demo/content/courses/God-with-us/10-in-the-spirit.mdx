## Session 10: In the Spirit

The descent of the Holy Spirit at Pentecost marks the beginning of another vital stage in the unfolding drama of Scripture. The account in Acts 1 and 2 is not a model for the experience of every individual believer, but the record of the coming of God at a defining moment in history.
