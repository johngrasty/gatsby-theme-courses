## Session 11: The Beginning of the End

Paul paints a picture of God’s cosmic plan with dramatic, sweeping strokes. He thinks in terms of two ages: ‘this age’, which is still around us, and ‘the age to come’ which has burst in with the coming of Jesus Christ.
