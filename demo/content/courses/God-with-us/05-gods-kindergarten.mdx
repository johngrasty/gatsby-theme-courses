## Session 5: God's Kindergarten

A major part of the first five books of the Bible is devoted to the account of the Tent of Meeting. This is a home for God among his people, an ‘embassy’ of his kingdom and a classroom where he teaches them about sin that separates, how access is made possible and about holiness as a lifestyle.
