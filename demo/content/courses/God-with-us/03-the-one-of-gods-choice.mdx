## Session 3: The One of God's Choice

The number ‘one’, as it is used in the Bible, gives us a striking insight into the uniqueness of the one God and how he works through one man, Adam; one people, Israel; one king, David and ultimately through one Lord, the Messiah, Jesus Christ. He always chose one for the sake of many.
